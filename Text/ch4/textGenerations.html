Why L1?

The text generation codes are written as function inside libraries by programmers. The generation through such a function is Level 1. The next is using Deep Learning Transformer models that is trained on millions of texts on the internet, and from the book. Then there is L3, which is artificial intelligence that writes to win the maximum resource or such sconres.
